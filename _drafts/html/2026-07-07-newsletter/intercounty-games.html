Inter-County Games

Congratulations to the Galway hurling team for their outstanding performance in the All-Ireland semi-final against Cork. They will face Limerick in the All-Ireland final on July 19th.

Ballinderreen N.S.'s Mr. Cooney scored a brilliant goal to seal the win.

The Galway camogie found out that they will play Kilkenny in the All-Ireland semi-final which takes place in Thurles on Saturday, July 25th at 4pm.

Congratulations to the Galway ladies football team for their excellent performance in the All-Ireland quarter-final against Mayo.

They will play Armagh in the All-Ireland semi-final on Saturday, July 18th at 3:15pm in Pearse Park, Longford. The game will be broadcast live on TG4.
