Inter-County Games

Commiserations to the Galway hurling team who lost the All-Ireland final against Limerick on Sunday.

Best of luck to the Galway camogie team who play Kilkenny in the All-Ireland semi-final in Semple Stadium, Thurles on Saturday at 4pm. Tickets can be purchased at this link: https://www.universe.com/events/glen-dimplex-championship-snr-semi-finals-2026-tickets-0B3VQ7.
