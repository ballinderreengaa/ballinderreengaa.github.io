Gort Show

The Gort Show will take place an August 8th and there are over 120 competitions to enter.

Schedules and entry forms are available to be picked up in Minihane’s SuperValu or Gort Credit Union. Otherwise, download the entry form or email gortshow@gmail.com.

Entry forms can be returned to Gort Town Hall between 10am and 4pm on the 23rd, 24th and 25th of July. They can also be dropped in to Giblin Trophies in an envelope marked "Gort Show" before Saturday, 25th of July.

Entry forms can be mailed to:

Gort Show Secretary
Giblin Trophies,
Glenbrack Road,
Gort, Co. Galway
H91 Y658

Details about the competitions can be found on the Gort Show website or on their Facebook page.
