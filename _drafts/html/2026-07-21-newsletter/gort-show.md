Over 120 competitions to enter this year at the Gort Show! We would love to see all the talented artists, photographers, bakers, writers and crafters in Gort and the surrounding areas! 
Schedules and entry forms are available to be picked up in Minihane’s SuperValu or Gort Credit Union. Otherwise, download the entry form (Facebook/ websites) or email gortshow@gmail.com.
Make sure to get your entry forms back to us in Gort Town Hall (H91 E0C8) on Thursday 23rd, Friday 24th or Saturday 25th July from 10-4.
Or drop in your envelopes labelled Gort Show to Giblin Trophies before Saturday the 25th.
If you do not live in Gort, please post your entry forms before the closing date to:

Calling all children in Gort, Beagh, Peterswell, Ardrahan, Kinvara, Tubber and North Clare! Are you good at drawing, painting, LEGO, baking, writing poems or taking photographs? Show off your entries at the Gort Show in the Children’s competition!   🎨🖼️✍️🧁 📸
Maybe you have a diorama project at home or you could make a robot from recycled materials on your home! 🤖Decorate a broken hurl or make an animal out of PlayDoh!🐷🐶 
Whichever you fancy, make sure you fill out an entry form in Gort Town Hall this week Thursday 23rd to Saturday 25th (10am- 4pm). You just need to let us know which classes you want to enter - entries will not need to be brought in until the evening before the Gort Show or the morning of the Show (Friday 7th August or Saturday 8th August). 📆📝✏️
If you cannot make the entry days, please post your entry form to Gort Show Secretary, Giblin Trophies, Glenbrack Road, Gort, Co. Galway before Saturday 25th July. ✉️📬
